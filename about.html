About me

As a Ph.D student delving into the exciting realm of AI and Medical Imaging, I am driven by a burning curiosity to uncover new ways to improve healthcare for all. The opportunity to conduct research that has real-world implications is a dream come true, and I am fully committed to using my skills and knowledge to make a meaningful impact.

My love for technology and science is matched only by my curiosity and eagerness to learn more. I am constantly seeking out new challenges and opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills. I am always looking to push the boundaries of what's possible, and I am confident that my PhD journey will take me to places I never thought possible.I am excited to see where this journey takes me and to be a part of the brilliant minds working towards shaping the future of healthcare through AI.

When I'm not poring over research papers and data, you can find me indulging in my other passions - travel, photography and music. From strumming my guitar to tinkling on the piano, music has always been my solace. ​Alongside that, I am passionate about travel and I consider it as one of my best way to escape reality and explore the world. I have captured many beautiful moments and memories through my camera lens and I am excited to share them with you.
