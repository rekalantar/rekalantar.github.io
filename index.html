Welcome to my website

Here, I showcase my journey as a deep learning scientist working on cutting-edge AI projects. ​You'll find a glimpse into my research experiences and the impact of my work in the field of medical imaging. Join me in the quest for improved cancer diagnosis and treatment through the power of AI.

Artificial intelligence (AI) holds great potential in the field of medical imaging, as it enables the early and precise diagnosis of diseases, thereby enhancing patient outcomes. One of the most challenging aspects of medical imaging is the segmentation of tumors, particularly those located in the pelvic region, which are often difficult to detect and isolate.

My Research

As a researcher, I am dedicated to exploring the latest generative models such as GANs and diffusion models to facilitate domain adaptation, along with advanced 3D techniques, such as transformers and attention neural networks, to enhance the segmentation of tumors on magnetic resonance imaging (MRI). By combining cutting-edge technology and rigorous scientific methods, I am committed to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the field of medical imaging, and ultimately improving patient care.
