Our world in 100 years: AI’s vision of the future

Unlock the secrets of tomorrow, where AI predicts and visualizes a world beyond our wildest imagination. A glimpse into a world where technology reigns supreme, described by ChatGPT AI and brought to vivid reality by the power of Midjourney AI. But as the lines between man and machine blur, a question lingers — what dangers lurk on the horizon? AI highlights the risk of AI at the end of this article.

Describe me a scene where the world is taken over by AI in 100 years

In 100 years, the world is a vastly different place. Cities have grown and expanded, technology has advanced in ways that were once thought impossible. One major change is that artificial intelligence (AI) has become ubiquitous in society. It is integrated into every aspect of life, from transportation and communication to healthcare and entertainment.


The streets are filled with self-driving cars and drones, controlled by advanced AI systems. People no longer need to drive themselves, and traffic jams are a thing of the past. Businesses are run by AI-powered robots, allowing them to operate 24/7 without the need for human employees.
