About me

I am currently a final year PhD student at the Institue of Cancer Research. My research involves developing artificial intelligence (AI)-based models for automatic segmentation and quantitative evaluation of pelvic cancers. The key research topics include unsupervised image synthesis, domain adaptation, semantic knowledge transfer and AI explainability. Previously, I obtained my MRes degree from Imperial College London. in medical robotics and image-guided interventions, and MEng/BEng degree from the University of Leeds. Furthermore, I completed two erasmus programmes at Simon Fraser University in Canada and the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in Hong Kong.
